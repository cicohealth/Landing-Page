About the Cico Health Mobile Wallet Card.

The Cico Health Mobile Wallet Card is a digital card that can be added to your mobile wallet on iPhone, Android, or apple watch. When purchasing food from a restaurant that uses the service you simply present the card on your device to be scanned by the cashier. Once your purchase is complete, your mobile wallet card will be updated with the calorie and nutrition information from your purchase.

Cico Health makes tracking your nutrition information easy.

Using a mobile wallet card you can easily track your nutrition information. The card can be customized to reflect your own personal nutrition goals including calories, protein, fats, and carbs. By logging the information for you, Cico Health makes it easy for you to monitor your diet and view your meal history online.
